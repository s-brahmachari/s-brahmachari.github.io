I am currently a Robert A. Welch Post-doctoral fellow at the Center for Theoretical Biological Physics, Rice University. Previously, I did my PhD from the Physics Department, Northwestern University. A physicist by training, my introduction to the world of cellular biology occured during my early PhD days, and thus started my journey thinking about the physical principles underlying biology.
